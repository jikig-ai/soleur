# The guided tour

The guided tour is a step-by-step spotlight walkthrough of the app's main areas, designed for your first visit.

## What it covers

The tour highlights each core area in turn — the dashboard, navigation sidebar, and key features — with a short explanation of what each is for. You advance step by step at your own pace.

## Using the tour

- **Next / Back** — move between steps.
- **Escape or close** — exit the tour at any time; nothing is lost.

## If you don't see the tour

The tour is being rolled out progressively, so it may not be enabled on your account yet. Everything it covers is also documented here in product help — ask the support chat about any specific area (for example "what is the Workstream tab?") and you'll get the same guidance.
