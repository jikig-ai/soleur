# Working with your AI agent (conversations)

Soleur's core idea: your company runs through conversations with an AI agent. You describe what you want in plain language; the agent does the work.

## Starting a conversation

- Press `g` then `c` (**Ask an agent**), or
- Open the command palette (**Ctrl+K** / **⌘K**) and choose **Ask an agent**.

## What the agent can do

The agent operates your company across domains — engineering, marketing, operations, product, and more. Typical asks:

- "Create an issue for the onboarding bug and put it in progress."
- "What's the status of my current work?"
- "Capture this decision in the knowledge base."
- "Research competitors for feature X."

Anything you can do in the app, the agent can do too — and it can see the same information you see, so you can refer to things naturally ("the item I just created").

## Replies

The agent streams its reply live as it works. Longer tasks show progress as they happen — you can keep the conversation open or navigate elsewhere and come back.

## Conversations vs. support chat

- **Ask an agent** is for doing work in *your* company.
- The **support chat bubble** (bottom corner) is for questions about *using Soleur itself*.
