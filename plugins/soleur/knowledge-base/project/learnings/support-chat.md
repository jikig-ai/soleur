# Getting help: the support chat

The **support chat bubble** in the bottom corner of the app is your 24/7 help line for questions about using Soleur.

## What to ask it

- How-to questions: "How do I create an issue?", "Where are my billing settings?"
- Navigation: "Where do I see what shipped recently?"
- Feature explanations: "What is a routine?"
- Troubleshooting: "The board won't load — what should I do?"

## What it can and can't do

- It **answers questions about the product** — grounded in the official product-help documentation.
- It **doesn't perform actions** in your account — for doing work (creating issues, editing documents), use **Ask an agent** (`g` then `c`) instead.
- It only discusses Soleur product help — it will politely decline unrelated topics.

## If the chat can't solve it

If your question isn't answered or something appears broken, say so in the chat — describe what you expected and what happened instead. Include the page you were on and any error message you saw.
