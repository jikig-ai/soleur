# Routines: recurring automated tasks

**Routines** (`g` then `r`) are tasks your agent runs for you on a schedule — daily digests, periodic checks, recurring reports — without you having to ask each time.

## Viewing your routines

The Routines tab lists your scheduled tasks with what they do and when they run.

## Managing routines

- Review a routine's schedule and recent activity from its entry in the list.
- To create a new routine or change one, ask your agent in a conversation — for example: "Every Monday morning, summarize last week's progress."

## Troubleshooting

- **A routine didn't run as expected:** check its entry in the Routines tab for recent activity, and ask the support chat if something looks wrong.
