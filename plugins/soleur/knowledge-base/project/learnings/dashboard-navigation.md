# Navigating the app: sidebar sections explained

The left sidebar is the main way to move between areas of Soleur. Every section also has a two-key "go to" shortcut: press **g**, then the letter.

| Section | What it's for | Shortcut |
|---|---|---|
| Dashboard | Your overview and home screen | `g` then `d` |
| Inbox | Items waiting on you | `g` then `i` |
| Workstream | Kanban board of your work items | `g` then `w` |
| CRM | Contacts board | `g` then `m` |
| Knowledge Base | Browse company documents and knowledge | `g` then `k` |
| Routines | Recurring automated tasks | `g` then `r` |
| Releases | Recent product updates | `g` then `l` |

## Settings and account areas

Open the command palette (**Ctrl+K** / **⌘K**) and choose **Settings** to reach:

- **All settings** — general preferences.
- **Team** — people in your workspace.
- **Billing** — your plan and payments.
- **Audit log** — a record of account activity.

## Tips

- You can collapse or expand the sidebar with the **Toggle sidebar** command in the palette.
- If you ever feel lost, press **?** to see the shortcut overlay, or open the palette and type where you want to go.
