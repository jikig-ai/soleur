# The Knowledge Base tab

**Knowledge Base** (`g` then `k`) is where your company's documents and accumulated knowledge live — organized as a browsable tree.

## Browsing

- The tree on the left lists your knowledge areas; click a folder to expand it and a document to read it.
- Documents are regular formatted text — headings, lists, tables, and links all render in place.

## How content gets here

Knowledge Base content is written and maintained by your AI agent as you work together — decisions, research, and documentation accumulate over time. You don't need to file documents manually; ask your agent to capture something and it lands in the right place.

## Tips

- Switching between tabs is instant — the app caches what you've already loaded, so flipping between Knowledge Base and other sections doesn't refetch everything.
- If you can't find something, ask your agent in a conversation — it can search the knowledge base for you.
