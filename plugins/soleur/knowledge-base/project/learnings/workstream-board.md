# The Workstream board (kanban)

**Workstream** (`g` then `w`) shows your work items as cards on a kanban-style board, organized in status columns so you can see what's planned, what's moving, and what's done at a glance.

## Reading the board

- Each **card** is a work item (an issue) with its title and key details.
- **Columns** represent status — items progress across the board as work advances.
- The board reflects the canonical status of your work, kept in sync with where the work actually happens, so what you see is current.

## Working with items

From the board you can:

- **Create** a new work item.
- **Update** an item — edit its details or move it to a different status.
- **Close** an item when it's done or no longer needed.

## Troubleshooting

- **The board looks empty or fails to load:** the app deliberately shows an error rather than a misleading empty board when it can't read your data. Retry in a moment; if it persists, tell the support chat.
- **An item is in the wrong column:** open it and change its status, or ask your agent to do it (see "Working with your AI agent").
