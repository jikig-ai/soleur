# Troubleshooting common issues

## I can't sign in

- Make sure you're at **app.soleur.ai** and using the email your account was created with.
- If you've never had access, you may still be on the waitlist — you'll be notified when your account is ready.

## A page won't load or shows an error

- Refresh the page — transient hiccups usually clear on reload.
- Soleur prefers showing an explicit error over silently showing you wrong or empty data, so an error banner means "try again," not "your data is gone."
- If the error persists, tell the support chat which page and what the message said.

## Keyboard shortcuts aren't working

- Shortcuts pause while a dialog is open or when focus is in a text field — click outside the field first.
- Press **?** to confirm the shortcut you're trying is the right one.

## My agent's reply seems stuck

- Long tasks stream progress as they run; give it a moment.
- You can navigate elsewhere and return — the conversation keeps its state.

## Something else is wrong

Describe the problem in the support chat: the page you were on, what you did, what you expected, and what happened instead. That's usually enough to get a useful answer immediately.
