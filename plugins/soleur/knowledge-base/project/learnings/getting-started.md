# Getting started with Soleur

Soleur is your AI-powered company command center. The web app lives at **app.soleur.ai**.

## Signing in

1. Go to app.soleur.ai — you will be redirected to the login page.
2. Sign in with your registered email account.
3. If you don't have access yet, join the waitlist from the Soleur website and you'll be notified when your account is ready.

## Your first look

After signing in you land on the **Dashboard**. The left sidebar is your main navigation:

- **Dashboard** — your overview and starting point.
- **Inbox** — items that need your attention.
- **Workstream** — your work items on a kanban-style board.
- **CRM** — your contacts board.
- **Knowledge Base** — your company's documents and knowledge.
- **Routines** — recurring automated tasks.
- **Releases** — what has shipped recently.

## Recommended first steps

- Take the **guided tour** if it's offered when you first arrive — it walks you through the main areas step by step.
- Press **?** (question mark) at any time to see all keyboard shortcuts.
- Press **Ctrl+K** (Windows/Linux) or **⌘K** (Mac) to open the command palette — the fastest way to move around and act.
- Use the **support chat bubble** (bottom corner) whenever you're stuck; ask it questions in plain language.
