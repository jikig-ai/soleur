# Settings, team, billing, and audit log

Account-level areas are reached through the command palette (**Ctrl+K** / **⌘K**) — choose **Settings**, then pick a destination:

## All settings

General preferences for your account and workspace.

## Team

The people in your workspace. Team availability may vary by plan and rollout stage.

## Billing

Your plan and payment details.

## Audit log

A chronological record of activity in your account — useful for reviewing what changed and when. Actions taken on your behalf (for example by your agent) are recorded here too, so there is always a trail.

## Common questions

- **"How do I change my plan?"** — Billing, via the palette's Settings entry.
- **"Who has access to my workspace?"** — Team.
- **"What changed recently in my account?"** — Audit log; for *product* changes, see the Releases tab (`g` then `l`).
