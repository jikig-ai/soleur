## Recitals

WHEREAS the Grantor operates a Soleur Web Platform workspace and holds a valid BYOK (Bring Your Own Key) API key registered with the Platform;

WHEREAS the Grantor wishes to fund the Grantee's AI agent runs within the shared workspace by delegating access to the Grantor's API key, subject to daily and hourly spending caps;

WHEREAS the BYOK delegation feature (`BYOK_DELEGATIONS_ENABLED`) routes the Grantee's prompt content through the Grantor's API key, creating a joint controllership relationship under Article 26 of the GDPR;

WHEREAS the Grantee must provide informed consent before the delegation becomes active, per Article 7 of the GDPR;

NOW, THEREFORE, the Parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. Scope of Delegation

1.1 The Grantor grants the Grantee access to the Grantor's API key for the purpose of running AI agent sessions within the shared workspace, subject to the spending caps set by the Grantor.

1.2 The delegation is revocable at any time by either Party. The Grantor may revoke by disabling the delegation in the team settings. The Grantee may decline or withdraw consent at any time.

1.3 The Grantor sets a daily spending cap (in USD) and an optional hourly cap. The Platform enforces these caps automatically.

---

## 2. Cost Telemetry Consent

2.1 The Grantee consents to the Grantor receiving **itemized cost telemetry** for each AI agent run funded by the delegation. This telemetry includes:
  - Token count (input and output)
  - Cost in USD cents
  - Timestamp of invocation
  - Agent role (domain leader identifier)

2.2 The Grantor does **NOT** receive access to:
  - The Grantee's prompt content
  - The AI assistant's response content
  - Any conversation history or context
  - Any personal data beyond what is listed in Section 2.1

---

## 3. Joint Controllership (Article 26 GDPR)

3.1 For the Grantee's prompt content routed through the Grantor's API key, the Grantor and Grantee are joint controllers within the meaning of Article 26 GDPR.

3.2 Anthropic PBC remains the processor under the Grantor's existing Data Processing Agreement. No new sub-processor relationship is created by the delegation.

3.3 Respective responsibilities:
  - **Grantor:** Responsible for the API key's security, spending cap management, and maintaining the Anthropic DPA.
  - **Grantee:** Responsible for the content of prompts submitted through the delegation.

---

## 4. Retention and Erasure

4.1 Delegation history (grant, revoke, cap changes) is retained for 7 years per financial audit requirements.

4.2 This consent record (acceptance row) is retained for 7 years.

4.3 On account erasure (Article 17 GDPR), both delegation and acceptance records are anonymised via the Platform's Art. 17 cascade. The Grantor's identity is removed; the Grantee's identity is removed. Audit-trail row counts are preserved.

---

## 5. Termination

5.1 Either Party may terminate this delegation at any time without cause.

5.2 Termination by the Grantor takes effect immediately (the Grantee's next agent run will fail with a "delegation revoked" error, with a 60-second grace window for in-flight sessions).

5.3 Termination by the Grantee removes the Grantee's acceptance. The delegation row remains but is inactive.

---

6.2 Disputes shall be submitted to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Paris (RCS Paris).
