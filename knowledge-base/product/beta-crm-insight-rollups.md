# Beta-CRM insight rollups (product) — see the canonical boundary note

`cpo` writes **aggregate, pseudonymised** product signal (recurring
pain-point / feature-request **themes**, stated with **no identifiable person**)
distilled from the owner-private beta-CRM here in `knowledge-base/product/`.

Raw third-party PII (`name` / `company` / verbatim conversation `body`) lives in
the database only and is **never** committed to git. The hard git/DB boundary,
the k-anonymity rule, and the deferred rollup-generation skill are documented in
the canonical note:
[`knowledge-base/sales/beta-crm-insight-rollups.md`](../sales/beta-crm-insight-rollups.md)
(ADR-102 §9, PA-30, LIA).
