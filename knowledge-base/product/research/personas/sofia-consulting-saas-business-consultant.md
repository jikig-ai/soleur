# Persona: Sofia

**Background:** Business consultant turned product founder. Built a client management SaaS for independent consultants using Bubble (no-code). Transitioned from billing clients hourly to building a product from her own pain.
**Revenue:** $1K MRR from 30 consultants paying $33/month
**Technical depth:** Low — no programming background. Built everything in Bubble. Hires freelance developers on Upwork for features she can't build in no-code. Understands product but not engineering.
**Primary pain:** Legal and compliance — she stores client data (some financial), has no privacy policy that actually matches her data practices, no DPA templates for enterprise prospects asking for them, and is terrified of a data breach she wouldn't know how to handle.
**Current tools:** Bubble for product, Notion for internal docs, ChatGPT Plus for drafting emails and proposals, Stripe for billing. No coding tools at all.
**AI attitude:** Enthusiast — uses ChatGPT daily for writing, planning, and brainstorming. "AI is the only way I can compete as a non-technical founder." Would try any tool that saves time.
