# Persona: Rafael

**Background:** Musician and audio engineer building a marketplace connecting independent musicians with sync licensing opportunities (music for ads, films, games). Pre-launch, self-funded from freelance audio work.
**Revenue:** $0 MRR (marketplace built with Webflow + Airtable, onboarding first 50 musicians)
**Technical depth:** Low — no programming skills. Built the entire platform using no-code tools (Webflow for frontend, Airtable for database, Zapier for automations). Hires Fiverr developers for custom integrations.
**Primary pain:** Legal and compliance — the sync licensing space is a legal minefield. He needs licensing agreement templates, rights management frameworks, royalty split contracts, and content moderation policies. Every musician he onboards asks "what happens to my rights?" and he doesn't have a real answer.
**Current tools:** Webflow, Airtable, Zapier, ChatGPT Plus (uses it daily for drafting emails, writing musician onboarding docs, and brainstorming feature ideas). No coding tools.
**AI attitude:** Pragmatist — relies on ChatGPT heavily for writing and admin tasks but knows it hallucinates legal details. "It's great for first drafts of everything except the stuff where getting it wrong could get me sued."
