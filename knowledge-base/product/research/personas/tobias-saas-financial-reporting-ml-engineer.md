**Current tools:** Windsurf for development, AWS for infrastructure, Figma for basic mockups (badly), Notion for notes. Uses Claude for brainstorming technical architecture.
**AI attitude:** Pragmatist — deeply understands AI capabilities and limitations from a technical perspective. Trusts AI for structured tasks, skeptical about AI for creative/strategic marketing because "it doesn't know my market."
