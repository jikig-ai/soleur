# Spec: Agentic Engineering Blog Post

## Problem Statement

The "agentic engineering" content space is crowded with definitions (IBM, Addy Osmani, Glide, Taskade), but no one has articulated the evolution from spec-driven development to compound engineering to full Company-as-a-Service. Soleur needs a pillar blog post that positions it as the most comprehensive compound agentic engineering system — demonstrating engineering depth AND cross-domain breadth.

## Goals

- G1: Publish a thought leadership blog post that ranks for "compound engineering" and "knowledge compounding" keywords
- G2: Position Soleur as the natural next step beyond spec-driven tools and engineering-only compound plugins
- G3: Provide concrete proof points from the knowledge base (210 learnings, worktree-write-guard arc, plan review scope reduction)
- G4: Drive engineering leaders and senior devs to evaluate Soleur for their teams

## Non-Goals

- NG1: Not a tutorial or how-to guide
- NG2: Not a product announcement or feature list
- NG3: Not a hit piece on competitors — "acknowledge then transcend" positioning
- NG4: Not a definition of "agentic engineering" — term is already established

## Functional Requirements

- FR1: Blog post follows the "Three Eras" framework (Vibe Coding → Agentic Engineering → Company-as-a-Service)
- FR2: Names competitors directly (Spec Kit, OpenSpec, Kiro, Tessl, Every) with honest assessment
- FR3: Includes concrete examples from Soleur's knowledge base as proof points
- FR4: Matches brand guide tone (manifesto + practitioner hybrid, bold, forward-looking)
- FR5: Never uses "plugin," "tool," "AI-powered," or hedging language per brand guide
- FR6: Includes comparison table showing capability coverage across competitors

## Technical Requirements

- TR1: Blog infrastructure needed — Eleventy docs site has no articles section yet
- TR2: SEO metadata: target keywords, meta description, Open Graph tags
- TR3: Canonical URL at soleur.ai/blog/three-eras-agentic-engineering/ (or similar)
- TR4: Social distribution plan (X/Twitter thread, Discord announcement)
