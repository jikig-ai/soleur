### Phase 1 — Draft the article (content-writer)

Invoke `content-writer` with this brief:

- **Title:** "Loop Engineering for Your Whole Company, Not Just Your Codebase" — **operator-confirmed 2026-06-12** after plan-review (DHH) proposed softening to "Beyond the Codebase." Deliberate brand-owner call: keep the hook; the body's honest hedge + non-affiliation disclaimer + blocking gates carry the accuracy. Do NOT re-litigate at review/work time. content-writer may refine wording for voice but MUST retain "whole company" as the hook.
- **Thesis:** credit Osmani's coinage, then extend cross-domain as the original contribution.
- **6-element mapping (honest hedge):** claim Worktrees, Skills, **MCP connectors**, External memory
  as company-wide today (external memory = the moat, lead with it). Frame Automations + maker/checker
  verifiers as **proven in engineering and extending outward** — declaratively, NOT with banned hedge
  words. Distinguish the 4 git-committed MCP servers (context7/cloudflare/vercel/stripe) from
  runtime-available ones ("available," not "shipped").
- **Block-4 rule:** call it "MCP connectors." NEVER frame Soleur as a "plugin" (brand-guide L96).
- **Credit-and-extend wall:** an attributed section (Osmani definition + verbatim Cherny/Steinberger
  quotes about the *term*) structurally separated from Soleur claims/CTAs, with an explicit
  **non-affiliation disclaimer**: "Soleur is not affiliated with or endorsed by Addy Osmani, Boris
  Cherny, Peter Steinberger, Google, or Anthropic."
- **Voice:** technical register; ambitious-declarative; proof points with **soft floors** ("60+
  agents", "60+ skills") never exact counts (Test 1). Avoid forbidden words: "AI-powered", "leverage
  AI", "just"/"simply", "assistant"/"copilot", "terminal-first"/"CLI-native", "plugin"/"tool" for
  Soleur, startup jargon.
- **No "open source" self-claim** (BSL 1.1 / source-available).
- **FAQPage (2+ Qs):** include "What is loop engineering?", "Can loop engineering work outside code?",
  "Is Soleur affiliated with Addy Osmani / Google / Anthropic?" (disclaimer in AEO-readable form). Any
  inline JSON-LD MUST use the `jsonLdSafe` filter, never `dump`.
- **Cross-link** to Soleur's existing `agentic-engineering` page (FROM the post body only).

content-writer's built-in Phase 2.5 citation gate runs here; Phase 3 is its approval gate.
