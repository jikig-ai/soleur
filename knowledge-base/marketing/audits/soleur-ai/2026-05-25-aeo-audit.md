# Soleur.ai AEO Audit — 2026-05-25

## Executive Summary

Soleur.ai has matured into a strong AEO-ready property. The `/company-as-a-service/` pillar page now anchors the site with named-expert attribution (Amodei, Altman, Krieger), quantitative third-party citations (Cursor $1B ARR/CNBC, Lovable $200M ARR/TechCrunch, Devin pricing/VentureBeat, WhatsApp/Instagram acquisition multiples), and a category-definition structure that AI engines can quote verbatim. The homepage and `/agents/` page carry concrete counts (67 agents, 8 departments, 77 skills) plus framework citations (Karpathy, Fowler, Feathers, Evans, Farley, DHH).

Remaining gaps cluster in **Presence**: third-party reviews and independent comparisons of Soleur itself (vs. competitors mentioning Soleur) remain thin, and there is no public citation-monitoring evidence captured in the audit trail. Authority is strong on the pillar but uneven across cluster pages — `/getting-started/` carries zero external citations. FAQ schema validity is owned by the seo-aeo-analyst (not assessed here), but FAQ section presence is consistently strong across audited pages.

## Key findings (prioritized by GEO impact)

1. **Source citations (highest GEO impact):** Pillar page is excellent. `/getting-started/` should add 2-3 inline citations to external sources (Anthropic docs for Claude Code, Apache-2.0 license link, methodology source for the 6-step workflow).
2. **Statistics / specificity:** Strong. Maintain soft-floor convention in prose ("60+ agents") and let templates render exact counts. Audit static pages for drift on next cycle.
3. **Presence (third-party):** Largest deficit. Seed at least one independent comparison or review surface — Product Hunt launch with comments, an HN Show post with discussion, or a G2/Capterra listing — before the next audit cycle so Presence can climb from 14/25 to ≥17/25.
4. **Definition extractability:** Add a one-paragraph plain-language definition of "Soleur" at the top of `/getting-started/` so AI engines can cite the product, not just the category.
5. **Entity clarity:** On `/company-as-a-service/`, separate the category definition section from the "Soleur as the leading CaaS platform" section so the category definition reads as neutral and quotable.

## Recommended fixes (next cycle)

- `/getting-started/`: add 2-line summary paragraph + 2-3 external citations (Apache-2.0 license, Claude Code docs, MCP spec). Estimated lift: Structure +2, Authority +2.
- `/company-as-a-service/`: split category definition from product positioning; remove or qualify "dramatically more productive than the first" and "leading CaaS platform" claims. Estimated lift: Authority +2, Conversational readiness +1.
- Presence: launch one independent surface (Product Hunt OR HN Show OR G2 listing) before 2026-06. Estimated lift: Presence +5 to +8.
- Citation monitoring: capture and store ChatGPT/Perplexity/Claude test-query results for "what is company-as-a-service", "AI agents for solo founders", "Soleur vs Cursor" in the next audit's evidence directory. No score lift directly, but unlocks Presence measurement.
