# Soleur.ai GEO/AEO Audit — 2026-05-04

Audit of soleur.ai for AI-engine consumability using the Structure / Authority / Presence (SAP) framework. Covers a sample of the most important pages: homepage, About, Vision, Pricing, Agents, and the blog index. Findings prioritize source citations > statistics > quotations > definitions > readability per the GEO impact ladder. Keyword-density signals are intentionally ignored (counterproductive for AI visibility).

## Top GEO findings, prioritized by impact

1. **Source citations are the binding constraint on Authority (P1).** Outside Pricing, the site leans on a single Inc.com article. Add 3-6 inline citations per pillar page (homepage, Vision, About) to: Anthropic primary sources for Claude / MCP, the MCP spec, Apache-2.0 license, and at least one independent industry source per departmental claim (e.g., a16z or Sequoia post on AI labor leverage; HBR or McKinsey on AI org design). Without external evidence, AI engines will keep citing Inc.com via Amodei and skip Soleur as the source.
2. **FAQPage JSON-LD must be confirmed on every Q&A block (P1).** WebFetch's rendered output did not surface FAQPage schema. Hand off to the seo-aeo-analyst agent to validate JSON-LD on /, /about/, /vision/, /pricing/, /agents/. If missing, FAQPage schema is the single highest-leverage AEO fix on the site — direct lift to AI Overviews and Perplexity citation eligibility.
3. **Add a "last updated" timestamp + author byline to every evergreen page (P1).** Homepage, About, Vision, Pricing, and Agents currently show only "© Soleur 2026". E-E-A-T weighting in AI ranking systems penalizes undated pages. Brand-guide attribution authority: Jean Deruelle (use exactly).
4. **Add a one-paragraph stat-led summary directly under the hero on every page (P2).** Hero slogans are skipped by extraction models. A 2-3 sentence factual paragraph stating "Soleur is a Company-as-a-Service platform: 66 agents, 8 departments, 67 skills, Apache-2.0 open-source, built on Claude Code and MCP" gives AI engines a clean quote target without diluting the brand hero.
5. **Place definitions adjacent to first usage on Vision (P2).** "The Global Brain", "The Decision Ledger", and "Master Plan" are coined terms used before being defined. Each needs a one-sentence quotable definition the first time it appears.
6. **Increase Presence via third-party surface area (P2).** Brand mentions in AI answers depend on third-party corpus presence. Concrete actions: ship the planned competitive-comparison cluster (vs Cursor, vs Devin, vs n8n, vs CrewAI), seed reviews on Product Hunt / GitHub Awesome lists / dev.to / HN, and start citation monitoring (test queries weekly in ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude.ai, Gemini for "AI agents for solo founders", "company as a service", "Soleur vs Cursor"). Track in `knowledge-base/marketing/audits/soleur-ai/citation-monitoring.md`.
7. **Source-link the authority names already used on /agents/ (P3).** Eric Evans, Michael Feathers, Dave Farley, Martin Fowler, DHH appear without links. One link per name to the canonical book / blog converts them from name-drops to citation-eligible claims.

## Brand-voice alignment notes

Start delegating."). All audited pages comply. The recommended stat-led summary paragraph (finding #4) should use the **General register** vocabulary on the homepage and About, and the **Technical register** on Vision and Agents — both registers already documented in the brand-guide.
