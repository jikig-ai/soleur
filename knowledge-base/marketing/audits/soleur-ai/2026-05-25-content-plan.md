# Soleur.ai Content Plan — 2026-05-25

## Executive Summary

Three audits converge on the same prescription: **fix the existing pages (P1 from content audit), then build the cluster pages that turn high-intent search traffic into pillar visits, then seed third-party Presence** (the 14/25 deficit in the AEO audit). The SEO foundation is already an A (95/100) — every plan in this document presumes that infrastructure (JSON-LD, robots.txt, sitemap, llms.txt) keeps working. The gap is **content surface area**: too few cluster pages, single-author byline, no comparison surface for the four highest-intent commercial queries ("Soleur vs Cursor", "Soleur vs Devin", "Soleur vs Lovable", "Soleur vs Agentforce").

Keyword research surfaced four cluster opportunities where competitor SERPs are dominated by listicles and weak comparisons — Soleur can win these with a category-owner voice and concrete proof. The plan classifies every piece as **searchable** (targets organic via keywords) or **shareable** (targets HN, X, LinkedIn, Discord). The mix is **60% searchable / 40% shareable** — flagging upfront that an all-searchable plan would forfeit the founder's strongest distribution lever (technical-register thought leadership on HN and X).
