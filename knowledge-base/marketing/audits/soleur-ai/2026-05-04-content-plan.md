# Soleur.ai Prioritized Content Plan ŌĆö 2026-05-04

This plan rolls the three audits forward into one ranked execution list. The site's classical SEO is already an A (97/100); the binding constraints are **Authority** (one external citation deep) and **Presence** (no third-party surface area), plus a handful of high-leverage rewrites called out in the content audit. ## 8. Brand voice / guide compliance gates
