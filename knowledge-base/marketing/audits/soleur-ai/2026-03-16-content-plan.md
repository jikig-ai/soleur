# Content Plan: soleur.ai

**Date:** 2026-03-16
**Scope:** Prioritized content plan based on content audit, AEO/GEO audit, keyword research, and competitor analysis
**Brand Guide:** All recommendations aligned against `knowledge-base/marketing/brand-guide.md`

## Executive Summary

This plan synthesizes findings from the 2026-03-16 content audit (3 critical issues, 11 improvements, 8 rewrite suggestions) and the AEO/GEO audit (overall grade B+, weakest areas: Vision page, Skills page, case studies) into a prioritized execution roadmap.

The plan addresses three strategic imperatives:

1. **Close AEO gaps immediately** -- FAQ sections, FAQPage schema, and source citations on core pages are blocking AI discoverability (P1).
2. **Capture uncontested keyword territory** -- "company-as-a-service," "solo founder AI tools," and "claude code plugin" have high volume with low-to-moderate competition. Soleur must establish content authority before competitors enter these terms (P1-P2).
3. **Build the content architecture for long-term dominance** -- Pillar/cluster linking, comparison articles, and shareable content are missing entirely. The current plan is 100% searchable; shareable content must be introduced (P2-P3).

**Content balance warning:** The existing site is 100% searchable content (keyword-targeted for search traffic). This plan introduces shareable content (opinion, data, narrative) in P2 and P3 to diversify distribution channels.

### 2C. Soleur's Defensible Content Moats

1. **"Company-as-a-service" -- Soleur owns this category.** No competitor uses the term. The CaaS pillar article has no competing definitions in search. This is a category-creation opportunity analogous to how Salesforce defined "no software" and HubSpot defined "inbound marketing."

2. **Multi-domain AI coverage** -- Every competitor (Cursor, Copilot, Windsurf, Devin) is code-only. Soleur's case studies covering legal, marketing, operations, competitive intelligence, and brand strategy have zero competition. No other tool can credibly write "how to generate legal documents with AI agents" or "how to run competitive analysis with AI."

3. **Solo founder positioning** -- While "solo founder AI tools" is a crowded listicle space, no tool claims to be the entire company. Soleur's "full AI organization" framing is unique. Competitors offer tools; Soleur offers an organization.

## 4. Content Scoring Matrix

Each content item is scored 1-5 on four dimensions. Total score determines priority ranking.

| Dimension | Definition |
|-----------|-----------|
| **Customer Impact** | Does this topic matter to the ICP (solo founders building at scale)? |
| **Content-Market Fit** | Can Soleur write this credibly given its product and expertise? |
| **Search Potential** | Keyword volume + keyword difficulty balance |
| **Resource Cost** | Inverse: 5 = very low effort, 1 = very high effort |

## 9. Risk Assessment

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Brand guide tension with "claude code plugin" keyword | Use "plugin" only in FAQ answers and meta descriptions where factual accuracy requires it. Keep H1s and hero copy plugin-free.
