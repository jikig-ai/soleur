## Discord

Every solo founder has the same problem: you context-switch away from operations for two weeks and the state is lost. Which hosting provider did you pick and why? What's the DNS configuration? When does the domain renewal hit? It lives in browser bookmarks, spreadsheets, and memory.

We built a full operations department with AI agents: a COO domain leader, an ops advisor, an ops researcher, and an ops provisioner.

What it produced:
- Structured expense tracking with provider, category, amount, renewal dates
- Domain and DNS inventory with record-level documentation and security audit
- Hosting decision (Hetzner CX22 at EUR 5.83/mo -- researched and evaluated, not just picked)
- Analytics decision (Plausible -- cookie-free, which simplified three legal documents by eliminating tracking cookie disclosures)
- Infrastructure security: Cloudflare strict SSL, HSTS preload, GitHub Pages wiring

The compound effect: the analytics decision simplified the Cookie Policy, GDPR Policy, and Privacy Policy. The expense tracker informed the business model evaluation. The COO auto-joins brainstorm sessions when operational decisions arise. No context reconstruction required when returning to ops after weeks of engineering.
