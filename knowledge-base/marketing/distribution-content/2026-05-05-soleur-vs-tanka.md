## Discord

Tanka and Soleur both claim "AI that never forgets." Same headline. Different machinery.

Tanka's EverMemOS captures what your team communicated — every Slack thread, email, Notion doc, WhatsApp message, searchable and retrievable. Communication-layer memory.

Soleur's knowledge base captures what was decided and why. When the legal agent documents a compliance requirement, the engineering agents reference it before building. When the brand guide is written, every marketing asset reflects it afterward. Execution-layer memory — it doesn't surface the past, it changes the future.

The practical difference: Tanka's memory is retrievable. Soleur's is generative.
