## LinkedIn Personal

Most AI comparisons miss the real question.

People benchmark Devin against other coding tools. Makes sense — it's an AI software engineer. Long-horizon coding tasks, autonomous execution, $20/month. Impressive.

But the comparison that matters for solo founders isn't Devin vs. another coding agent. It's: does an AI software engineer solve the problem I actually have?

Most solo founders aren't bottlenecked on engineering velocity. They're running 9 jobs — engineering is one of them. The others are still manual.

I wrote a comparison between Devin and Soleur (what I'm building) that tries to be honest about when each is right.

Devin wins if you have a pure engineering problem with validated product, legal/financial infrastructure in place, and the constraint is shipping code faster. That's a real scenario — some companies genuinely are that simple.

Soleur covers the rest: 65 agents, 9 departments, one compounding knowledge base. Legal, finance, marketing, operations — the functions that eat founder time but don't get an AI tool at $20/month.

The conclusion isn't "Devin is wrong" — it's that an AI software engineer and an AI organization solve different problems. #solofounder #buildinpublic
