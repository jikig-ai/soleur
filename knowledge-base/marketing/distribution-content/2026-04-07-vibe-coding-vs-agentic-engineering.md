## Discord

New article on the shift that solo founders can't ignore.

Andrej Karpathy coined vibe coding in February 2025: describe what you want, accept the AI's output, iterate fast. One year later, he introduced agentic engineering -- the practice of orchestrating AI agents with human oversight.

The structural difference comes down to three things:
→ Specification before execution -- you and the agent both know what done looks like
→ Verification gates -- automated review at every stage of the workflow
→ Persistent knowledge -- learnings that survive sessions and enforce themselves

For a team, the "no memory" problem gets absorbed through human coordination. The senior dev remembers architectural decisions. QA catches regressions. Code review creates accountability.

Solo founders have none of those redundancies. Agentic engineering is how you replace them.
