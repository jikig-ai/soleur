## Discord

Notion Custom Agents exited beta on May 3. Credit pricing is live. $10 per 1,000 Notion credits + $20/seat Business plan. 21,000+ agents built during the beta. 2,800 running at Notion internally.

We went through the comparison again. The architecture gap has only widened.

Notion automates recurring tasks on triggers — schedule fires, Slack message arrives, database row changes, agent runs. Standups, triage, status reports. Great for teams with operational overhead. Designed for teams, not solo founders.

Soleur orchestrates complete business lifecycle workflows with compounding knowledge. Engineering features run through specification → architecture review → implementation → security audit → knowledge capture. Legal reviews reference the privacy policy automatically. Competitive analysis shapes pricing strategy. Every session teaches the system.

The deeper difference: Notion agents read from your workspace. One domain's decisions don't automatically inform another. Soleur's knowledge base compounds across every session and every domain — the 100th session is categorically more capable than the first.

Pricing: Notion is $20/seat/month + $10/1,000 credits. Soleur is open source, Apache-2.0, free.
