We went through the comparison. The architectures are different enough that it's two different categories.

Notion automates recurring tasks on triggers — schedule fires, Slack message arrives, database row changes, agent runs. Standups, triage, status reports. High value for teams with operational overhead.

Pricing: Notion is Business plan ($20/seat) + credits ($10/1,000) after the beta ends May 3. Soleur is open source, Apache-2.0, free.
